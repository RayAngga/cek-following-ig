Cara mengunduh data dari Instagram

  1. Buka Instagram → Profil.
  2. Tap (☰) → masuk ke Pusat Akun.
  3. Pilih Informasi dan izin Anda.
  4. Pilih Ekspor informasi Anda → Buat ekspor.
  5. Pilih profil yang ingin kamu ekspor.
  6. Pilih Ekspor ke perangkat.
  7. Sesuaikan format dan tanggal yang ingin kamu ekspor → di menu Sesuaikan informasi → pilih Koneksi → centang Pengikut & Mengikuti.
  8. Klik Mulai ekspor → tunggu beberapa saat sampai muncul Download.
  9. Ekstrak ZIP → buka folder followers_and_following → gunakan followers_1.json & following.json di halaman ini.
  1. Open Instagram → go to your Profile.
  2. Tap (☰) → go to the Accounts Center.
  3. Select Your information and permissions.
  4. Choose Download your information → Create export.
  5. Select the profile you want to export.
  6. Choose Export to device.
  7. Adjust the format and date range you want to export → in Customize information → choose Connections → check Followers & Following.
  8. Click Start export → wait a few moments until the Download appears.
  9. Extract the ZIP → open followers_and_following → use followers_1.json & following.json on this page.
